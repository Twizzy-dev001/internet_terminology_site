TCP/IP

Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) is a set of communication protocols used to connect devices on the internet. IP is responsible for addressing and routing data packets so they reach the correct destination, while TCP ensures that the data is delivered accurately, in the correct order, and without errors. Together, they form the foundation of how data is transmitted across networks.
